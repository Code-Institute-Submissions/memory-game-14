Simon Game!

Welcome to my Simon Game, this was a classic game back in the early eighties and now is still being used today along with other versions of this including the popular game "Bop It"©

Instructions

In this game you will follow the sequence of colours and every round will get higher and higher, your score will be shown so try to reach a high score.

If any of you think this is too easy, then there is a Strict mode you can turn on which if you make a mistake you will lose your score and have to start again!

So if you think your ready to play click the button below to go to the game.
